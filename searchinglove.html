Searching Love

2d animation

(2024)

“Searching Love” explores the complexity of love and the idea that, for many, it can always seem out of reach. Through the story of a character grappling with an insatiable search for this feeling, I wanted to convey the emptiness and loneliness that accompany that quest. I hope the animation resonates even with people at different stages of their lives, since everyone, in some way, has dealt with the presence or absence of love. The search for it can seem difficult and impossible, and we often believe it isn’t meant for us—and it is precisely this universality that I wanted to capture.
