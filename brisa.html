Brisa de Verão

2d animation

(2025)

“Summer Breeze” drifts through the seasons and memories, exploring the longing and remembrance of someone who is no longer here. In this project, we follow the evolution of emotions over time, as each season reveals a new layer of feeling—from farewell to blossoming, from absence to nostalgia. Nature and time mirror our soul, and there are moments when the breeze brings us back to the past—gentle and unexpected, like a voice whispering things we can no longer put into words. And sometimes, without meaning to, we fly away with it, even without knowing if we’re ready to leave.
